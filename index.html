Who We Are

Vellore's Most Trusted Electrical Partner

TamilNadu Electronics is an electrical components and home appliances company with a strong focus on customer satisfaction. We provide high-quality branded home appliances through our showroom, where our range of products and prices are unbeatable.

We have a large showroom in Vellore where we display all our products. Customers can come and view the products and there is plenty of seating. We supply several colleges, mini-hospitals, apartment complexes and builders with electrical fixtures — and we are also a major supplier for some of the biggest builders in the district.
